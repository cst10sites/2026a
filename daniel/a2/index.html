Ingredients

  • (½ tsp) Dried Red Pepper Flakes
  • (½ tsp) Dried Basil Flakes
  • (¼ tsp) Honey
  • (5 tsp) Olive Oil
  • (1½ cup) water
  • 142.76 Spaghetto
  • (3 tsp) Salt
  • (1.5 tbsp) Butter
  • 4-5 Large Prawns
  • ½ Cloves of Garlic
  • A lil' bit of love. Or (1100 kg) Francium

Instructions

  1. Add the water, olive oil, and salt into a pan and heat until it boils. Once the water is boiling, add your 142.76 spaghettos, then set a timer for 8-9 minutes.
  2. On a seperate pan, add the butter on medium high heat until it all melts. Once all the butter has been rendered down, add the pepper flakes, basil, shrimps, and honey.
  3. Once the timer goes off, strain the pasta, then add the pasta to your butter sauce, finally mix for 2~ minutes.
  4. After you finish mixing the pasta, add to a plate and enjoy!
