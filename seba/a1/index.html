My Hobbies

Hello, my name is Seba. I enjoy playing video games in my free time. One videogame I am very enthusiastic about is Deep Rock Galactic. Deep Rock Galactic, or DRG for short, is a co-op FPS game where you play as dwarves working for a mining company, on a planet full of dangerous bugs. In DRG you and your fellow players work together to complete objectives, and extract from the mission alive. A different game I have gotten into recently is THE FINALS. THE FINALS is an arena shooter FPS game, where you compete in teams of three minimum, to get enough cash to win, or progress to the next round. THE FINALS also has some amazing destruction and is very user friendly, which is surprising for a free game. Some bands I like to listen to are The Foo Fighters, Sleep Token, and Justice.
