The classes of DRG

There are four classes in DRG, all with thier own unique roles, playstyles ,primary and secondary weapons, throwables, gadgets and traversal tools! We will be taking a look into each class, showing all that makes the class unique from the others. It will go in the order of playstyle/role, then wapons and gear. We will only go over the default weapons and thier inital stats, default grenade, and the inital gear stats. All dwarves have flares that will recharge over time, a head lamp, a terrain scanner to help find things, and a lazer pointer/scanner/ping tool.All of the stat info was taken from the Deep Rock Galactic Wiki.

Gunner:

Overview:  The Gunner class is the main dealer of consistent damage, all of his primmary weapons are automatic, with single target, big damage, secondary weapons. If you like having the bigest guns, and being the one everyone can rely on, then gunner is the class for you.

Weapons And Gear:  The first weapon that the gunner has is the "LeadStorm" Powered Minigun. the "LeadStorm" is a large minigun that has a large ammo cpacity and a high rate of fire. It takes a second to start firing, and gets to full accuracy after firing for about a second. The "LeadStorm" also works on a overheat system, where the more you shoot, the hotter it gets, and will lose heat after not firing for a few seconds, and will jam and not shoot for a few seconds if it overheats. You will also move at half speed when firing. The "LeadStorm" will also use 2 ammo for every 1 bullet fired, and has a chance to stun enemies it hits.

Name: "LeadStorm" Powered Minigun
Damage: 10 (kenetic)
FireRate: 15 (30)
Ammo: 1200 (2400)
Cooling Rate: 1.5
Cooling Delay 0.3s
Spinup Time: 0.7
Spindown Time: 2

Overheat Duration: 10s

Gunner's second weapon is the "Bulldog" Heavy Revolver. It's a large revolver that shoots large bullets that deal big damage, reasonably it only holds four shots, and has high amounts of stun.

The traversal tool that gunner has is the Zipline Launcher. The Zipline Launcher dose as the name sugests and makes a zipline from where you are standing, to where you are aiming. The zipline has limited range, and a limited angle at which you can place it. You move along the zipline slowly if the zipline isn't upgraded, but you can go faster when moving down at an angle. In order to place the zipline you must be on solid ground. Any player can youse the zipline, and you can switch directions on th zipline by pressing the interact button.

The gunner's final piece of kit is his handy Shield Generator. The Shield generator will project an enrgy field around itself, that will stop most enemies from entering the field, and will block any projectiels from coming inside the field. The player's personal shield will replenish while inside the field, and players take 50% less damage while inside. Players can shoot through the field while inside it, but not from the outside. When the shield duration is finneshed, the Generator will fly back to the player, and will begin to recharge.

Scout:

Overview:   The Scout is the most mobile of the classes, and moves quickly with his grappling hook. He has some more generic weaponry thet people might recognise, and good crowd control grenades. If you like moving fast, and don't mind the occasional fall damage, then the Scout is the class for you.

Weapons And Gear:   The scout's default primary weapong is the DeepCore GK2. A normal full-auto assault rifle with a satisfying firing sound, and true relieability. It has a chance to stun enemies when hitting thier weakspot.

Any enemy caught in the field will be slowed down, and will be considered to be electrocuted. Enemies will also take more damage in the field.

The scout's amazing traversal tool, the Grppling Hook. The grapple hook will launch to the target location, before pulling the player there after a short delay. If the trigger is held, the player will continue towoards the target location, when there the player will be suspended in place. When the trigger is released, the player will fall, and the recharge will begin. The player can releas the trigger before reaching the location to stop moving towoards the location. If aimed and shot at the ground, even while falling, the player will accelerate to the ground without taking fall damage.

The scout's final gadget is his Flare Gun. While all classes have basic flares that they can throw to give some light, the flare gun fires much brighter flares, that stick into surfaces. The flare projectile will deal damage if it hits an enemy, but will not stick to the enemy and will just fall. If the angle at which it hits a surface is too extreme, or the terrian suporting it is broken, the flare will fall.

Engineer:

Overview:   The Engineer is the most tactical, and defense oriented of the classes. He can add platforms to the terrain with his platform gun, and rain support fire with his sentry gun. If you enjoy thinking, strategy, and holding your ground, then the Engineer class is for you.

Weapons And Gear:   The engineer's first primary weapon is the "Warthog" Auto 210. The "warthog" is a reliable semi-automatic shotgun. The gun uses a magazine, and has a decent chance of stunning enemies.

It fires a projectile grenade, thats affected by gravity, which also destroys terrain. It only holds one grenade at a time. The grenade explosion deals less damage the further the enemy is from the point of impact, and can scare enemies.

Most enemies will be attracted to and attack the L.U.R.E., but bigger and stronger enemies are less likely to be fooled. The hologram will move from a greenish-yellow color, to a more red color, as it takes damage. When the hologram is destroyed, it will release an electric shock wave. The engineer's most iconic piece of kit, the LMG Gun Platform (Sentry Gun). The Sentry is an autonomous sentry gun/turret that will shoot any enemies it sees, even if it can't damage it from that angle. When placed, the sentry will be in an unbuilt state, and must be set up before it is operational, any player can assist in building the sentry. The sentry cannot be placed on wall, only flat, solid surfaces. If the terrain the sentry is on is destroyed, the sentry will float in the air. The sentry has it's own ammo capacity, and will consume ammo while shooting, so you must refill the sentries ammo. The engineer has his own senty ammo reserve, and if there is another engineer on your team, they can refill your sentries with thier own reserve. The sentry gun has a limmited range at which it can shoot enemies. you can call back your sentry by holding the reload button. if you select to place a sentry when you already have it placed down, it will deconstruct itself, and travle to the new location. Sentry gun ammo can be reffiled with resupplies.
