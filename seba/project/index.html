General Theme/Concept

Deep Rock Galactic is a mission based FPS game made by Ghost Ship Games where you play as space dwarves who work for the mining company Deep Rock Galactic. You and up to three other players are sent into the caves of HoxxesIV, the most mineral dense, but also the most dangerous planet in the galaxy. In the caves you will meet hordes of deadly bugs who want nothing more than to tear you apart. You must use your weapons to defend yourself, your tools to travers the caves, and your trusty pickaxe to mine any shiny minerals you find. You must also remember that your not here just to shoot bugs and mine gold, you have objectives to complete, and once completed you can leave via the drop pod, after the minerals have been secured of course.

General GamePlay

In DRG you can play as one of four classes, Driller, Gunner, Scout, and Engeneer. Each have thier role to play in the team, and have thier own respective strengths and weaknesses. After you choose the class you want to play as for the mission, you get into the drop pod in the space rig, and get sent into the depths of HoxxesIV.
There are nine missions to choose from, ranging from simple mining missions, to extracting liquid morkite from wells, through pipelines, to a refinery. Point is there are many missions to chose from, so lets have an example of how a mission might go.

Your First Morkite Mission

You and your team (you are a Gunner in this mission) drop into the cave with a bang, imedeatly leaving the drop pod to get to work, with the M.U.L.E tagging not far behind. Your Driller digs through the dirt blocking the entrance to the next cave section. After some exploring of the cave, you see a glint of light from your flare coming off of a shiny dark green mineral, Morkite. You go to mine it with your Pickaxe, but you hear the roar of Glyphid grunts coming to make you thier lunch. With fast thinking you blast them with your minigun till there is nothing left of them, and go on to mine the morkite in relative peace. After that you meet up with your team, and deposite the minerals into the M.U.L.E . Some time has passed and your team has mined some Nitra(you can use Nitra to call a resuply pod with health and ammo), but dosen't need a supply pod right now. Eventualy you get through to the last cave sction when Mission Control warns you of an impending Swarm of angry Gliphyds! You and your team quickly prepare and befor you know it, there are at least a hundred large bugs with sharp teath.
You and your team fight valiantly, but it's too much for your Scout, and he goes down. You quickly throw down your bubble shield, and revive your team mate. After finishing off whats left of the swarm, your team gathers the last of the Morkite, and deposites it in the M.U.L.E . A big button apears on the back of the mule, and you call down the drop pod to extract. After a quick dash to the pod, being chased by even more Gliphids, you and your team must hold out one last time at the drop pod, while the M.U.L.E slowly treads to the pod. After the drop pod doors open up, and you and your team get inside, you give the good salute of "Rock And Stone!"
