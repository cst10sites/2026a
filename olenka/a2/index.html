My recipe - Fettuccine Alfredo

Introduction:

Fettuccine Alfredo, a classic italian pasta dish that is creamy and full of flavor. I chose this recipe because it's simple to make, but tastes so so delicious. I like this recipe as it's easy to cook, can find in almost every restaurant and always has a perfect taste to it.

Ingredients:

    Prep Time: 10min     Cook Time: 15min     Total Time: 25min     Servings:4
  • Fettuccine pasta - 24 ounces
  • Butter - ½ cup
  • Heavy cream - 1 cup
  • Salt and pepper
  • garlic salt - 1 dash
  • Parmesan cheese - 1 cup, grated

Instructions

  1. Fill a large pot with water, add a pinch of salt, and wait for it to boil and be around 100°C.
  2. Add the fettuccine pasta and cook according to the package instructions.
  3. Drain the pasta and set aside, reserving about 1/2 cup of the pasta water.
  4. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat.
  5. Add the heavy cream to skilley and stir until combined.
  6. Slowly whisk in the grated parmesan cheese until the sauce is smooth and creamy.
  7. Season the sauce with salt and black pepper to taste.
  8. Add the cooked Futteccine pasta to the skillet and toss to coat in the Alfredo sauce.
  9. Serve hot and if you'd like you could add extra pasmesan for better taste.
