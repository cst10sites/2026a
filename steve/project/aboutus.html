About us

Founded in 2025, FindMyTutor is a student-led, non-profit tutoring organization committed to providing free, high-quality academic support to young learners. Built on the principle of “students guiding students,” we aim to make education more accessible and supportive for children in preschool and primary school. Our organization was created with a clear purpose: to help students reach their full potential regardless of their background or circumstances.

Our tutors are dedicated volunteers, primarily high school and university students from Grade 10 and above, who bring both academic competence and genuine commitment to teaching. We go beyond simple homework help. We focus on building meaningful relationships with students, acting as mentors who encourage curiosity, resilience, and confidence. By fostering a supportive and welcoming learning environment, we strive to make education a positive and empowering experience rather than a source of stress.

At FindMyTutor, we value connection, consistency, and care. We believe that when students feel understood and supported, meaningful learning follows.

Our Mission

At FindMyTutor, our mission is to ensure that every child has the opportunity to succeed academically. We provide free tutoring by connecting compassionate and capable student volunteers with elementary-level learners who need additional support in core subjects such as reading, writing, and mathematics.

Our goal is not only to improve academic performance, but also to help students develop confidence, independence, and a strong foundation for lifelong learning. By removing financial barriers and emphasizing mentorship, we aim to create equal educational opportunities and make a lasting, positive impact on students’ educational journeys.
