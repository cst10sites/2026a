Welcome to the official FindMyTutor website, a student-led, non-profit platform designed to make free academic support accessible to young learners. This website serves as the central hub for learning about our organization, our values, and the services we provide.

Through the homepage, visitors can gain a clear overview of FindMyTutor’s mission and impact. The About Us page outlines who we are, what we stand for, and how our student volunteers work together to support elementary-level students. Users can create an account through the Sign Up and Register pages, allowing them to join our community as tutors and contribute to meaningful educational support. The Log In page provides returning users with secure access to their accounts and platform features.

Together, these features ensure a clear, organized, and user-friendly experience for anyone interested in learning about FindMyTutor or becoming part of its mission to support students through free tutoring.
