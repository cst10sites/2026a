Season 2026 Vehicles Technology Innovations -- RB22

We still know little about Oracle Red Bull Racing's new model in 2026 - the RB22, since the first race is in March, 2026.
The following is what we know for now:

Differences in outer appearance and design:

  1. Size: The model is significantly relatively smaller and lighter, as shown in the graph below.

    2. Specification RB18 (2022 Red Bull Racing) RB22 (2026 Red Bull Racing)

  2. Aerodynamics: The DRS (Drag Reduction System, when driver opens a flag on the rear wing to change the aerodynamic drag) is abandoned, replaced by "Overtake Mode" and active aerodynamics (movable front/rear wings).

  3. Suspension: major design shift with a double-pushrod front suspension, a great move-away from the current front-pullrod and rear-pushrod suspension system.
