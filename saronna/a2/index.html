My Recipe

Hate is a strong word, but im allowed to use it when it comes to cooking. Many people think im joking when I say im a "Chef", but I really do know how to cook. There's one thing that really annoys me is when people boil meat. I know that you can vacum seal some meats and sort of steam them, but straight boiling them in water ruins the meat. That's why the first thing I tell people I can cook steak, because people over estimate how well they can cook steak (Grodon Ramsay's way). For this recipe you will need a pan, cutting board, a knife and a spatula, and seasonings.

Prep

**Wash your hands before cooking...I see you

What to use for the best results:

  • You can use any kind of protien, but it holds better it its a fillet
  • Butter makes the meat tender, but if you're trying to be healthy Olive oil is a good option (it can handle higher heat to)
  • Its best to wash the brussel sprouts (soak in salt water, optionally)
  • Use two pans so you can finish at the same time

Cooking:

  1. Turn the stove, while it's heating up salt and pepper the steak (don't over pepper!)
  2. Wash about two cups of brussels and then soak them in salt water
  3. Gently place the steak in the heated pan. This part is just to fast sear the sides of the steak to get that irresistible crisp
  4. Now rinse the brussels and get your second pan with the olive oil salt and pepper
  5. Toss you brussels and turn the heat down for the steak
  6. The steak should only take a few minutes on each side (if you want it to be medium rare)
  7. Turn the heat off on the steak and let the brussels stay on the heat
  8. After a few minutes take everything off the heat and serve with your choise of drink

Enjoy! :)
